## ‚úÖ WHY THIS PALETTE WORKS FOR FINANCIAL TRAINING

### Professional Foundation:
- **Primary Blue (#003B73):** Finance industry standard, conveys trust
- **Gold (#C9A961):** From your logo, represents achievement and premium quality
- **Neutrals:** Clean, professional, easy to read

### Vibrant Accents:
- **Teal (#00B4A8):** Modern, energetic, differentiates from typical finance sites
- **Coral (#FF6B6B):** Strategic urgency without being aggressive
- **Purple (#6B4C9A):** Premium positioning for advanced offerings

### Balanced Approach:
- 75% professional (blues, golds, neutrals)
- 25% vibrant (teal, coral, green)
- Result: Trustworthy yet modern and engaging

### Psychology:
- **Blue:** Trust, authority, competence (essential for finance)
- **Gold:** Achievement, excellence, premium (from your brand)
- **Teal:** Innovation, growth, forward-thinking (appeals to modern professionals)
- **Purple:** Prestige, wisdom (perfect for advanced certifications)
