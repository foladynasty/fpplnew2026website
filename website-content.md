### Affiliations
*   **Financial Planning Standards Board Ltd (FPSB):** The Financial Planning Standards Board Ltd (FPSB) is a U.S.-based non-profit association that manages, develops and operates certification, education and related programs for financial planning organizations to benefit the global community by establishing, upholding and promoting worldwide professional standards in personal financial planning. FPSB owns the CFP®, CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ marks and awards them to individuals who meet FPSB's initial and ongoing certification requirements.
*   **Financial Planning Association Of Singapore (FPAS):** FPAS is a non-profit professional association dedicated to developing and promoting an industry providing unbiased financial advice to the Singaporean public. Since 1st December 1998, its vision is to ensure that all Singaporeans have access to responsible and appropriate financial planning advice by raising the professional standards of the industry through education and a shared code of ethics. FPAS is part of a global assembly of financial planning bodies, and a licensee of the Financial Planning Standards Boards Ltd (FPSB). Launched in October 2004, its aim is to set standards and oversee the international CFP® certification program. As such, FPAS oversee the licensing process for the CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™, Associate Wealth Planner and Associate Financial Planner.

## CFP® Certification Page

### Curriculum Modules
1.  **Module 1: Foundations in Financial Planning** (40 hours)
    *   Description: Build a solid foundation in financial planning principles, ethics, and professional standards.
    *   Topics: Introduction to Financial Planning Process, Professional Ethics and Standards of Conduct, Regulatory Framework in Singapore, Client Communication and Relationship Management, Time Value of Money and Financial Mathematics.
2.  **Module 2: Risk Management and Insurance Planning** (40 hours)
    *   Description: Master insurance planning and risk management strategies for comprehensive client protection.
    *   Topics: Risk Assessment and Management Techniques, Life Insurance Planning and Needs Analysis, Health and Medical Insurance Strategies, Disability and Long-Term Care Planning, Property and Liability Insurance.
3.  **Module 3: Tax Planning and Estate Planning** (40 hours)
    *   Description: Understand tax-efficient strategies and estate planning for wealth preservation and transfer.
    *   Topics: Singapore Income Tax System & Planning, Business and Corporate Tax Planning, Estate Planning and Wealth Transfer, Wills, Trusts, and Probate, Succession Planning for Business Owners.
4.  **Module 4: Investment Planning** (40 hours)
    *   Description: Develop expertise in investment strategies, portfolio management, and asset allocation.
    *   Topics: Investment Vehicles and Asset Classes, Modern Portfolio Theory and Asset Allocation, Equity and Fixed Income Analysis, Alternative Investments and Derivatives, Performance Measurement and Portfolio Rebalancing.
5.  **Module 5: Retirement Planning** (40 hours)
    *   Description: Learn comprehensive retirement planning strategies for long-term financial security.
    *   Topics: Retirement Needs Analysis, CPF Optimization Strategies, SRS and Private Retirement Schemes, Retirement Income Streams, Managing Longevity Risk.
6.  **Module 6: Financial Plan Construction & Professional Responsibilities** (40 hours)
    *   Description: Integrate all planning areas to create comprehensive financial plans for diverse client scenarios.
    *   Topics: Financial Planning Case Analysis, Comprehensive Plan Development, Client Presentation and Communication, Implementation and Monitoring Strategies, Practice Management and Professional Responsibility.

## SCFP-HNWI Certification Page

### Program Overview
*   **Title:** Specialist Certificate in Financial Planning - High Net Worth & Family-Owned Business (SCFP-HNWI)
*   **Description:** An advanced certification for experienced financial advisors who want to specialize in serving ultra-high-net-worth individuals and family enterprises.
*   **Key Benefits:** Serve Singapore's Wealthiest, Command Premium Fees, Exclusive Positioning, Family Business Opportunities.

### Modules List
1.  **Module 1:** HNWI Market Segmentation & Client Acquisition (Core)
2.  **Module 2:** Family Business Advisory & Succession Planning (Core)
3.  **Module 3:** Advanced Wealth Structuring & Estate Planning (Core)
4.  **Module 4:** HNWI Investment & Portfolio Management (Core)
5.  **Module 5:** Ethics, Compliance & Professional Standards (Core)
6.  **Elective A:** Gig Economy & Non-Traditional Income Planning
7.  **Elective B:** HNWI Retirement & Longevity Planning

### Detailed Module Content

#### Module 1: Segmentation & Targeting for Effective Positioning & Propositions
*   **Focus:** Client acquisition frameworks, personal branding, value positioning for HNWI.
*   **Challenge:** Generic approaches don't work with sophisticated wealthy clients.
*   **Solution:** Develop targeted strategies and value propositions that resonate.
*   **Key Topics:** Personal Branding, Market Segmentation, Value Proposition Development, Customer Acquisition Strategies.
*   **Duration:** 2 Days (14 hours).
*   **CPD:** 14 Non-core Supplementary Hours.

#### Module 2: Strategies for Advising High-Net-Worth Individuals
*   **Focus:** Relationship building, product suitability, multi-generational planning.
*   **Challenge:** Understanding unique psychology and complex needs of HNWIs.
*   **Solution:** Master product breadth, build trust, handle complex conversations.
*   **Key Topics:** HNWI Psychology, Product Knowledge & Suitability, Complex Advisory Solutions, Relationship Building.
*   **Duration:** 1 Day (7.5 hours).
*   **CPD:** 7.5 Non-core Supplementary Hours.

#### Module 3: Products Propositions and Structures for Wealthy Individuals
*   **Focus:** Bespoke wealth management structures, insurance, investment solutions.
*   **Challenge:** Complex wealth structuring needs requiring sophisticated knowledge.
*   **Solution:** Master UL/VUL/IUL, PPLI, Trusts, EAM, and alternative investments.
*   **Key Topics:** Advanced Product Structures, Trust & Estate Structures, Investment Solutions, Product Structuring & Analysis.
*   **Duration:** 2 Days (14 hours).
*   **CPD:** 14 Non-core Supplementary Hours.

#### Module 4: Financial Planning for SME Businesses
*   **Focus:** Operational risk, business continuity, succession, exit planning.
*   **Challenge:** Unique financial challenges of business owners (accounting, risk).
*   **Solution:** Corporate accounting principles, financial ratio analysis, bespoke SME solutions.
*   **Key Topics:** Corporate Financial Analysis, Risk Management & Protection, Business Planning, Product Solutions for SMEs.
*   **Duration:** 2 Days (14 hours).
*   **CPD:** 14 Non-core Supplementary Hours.

#### Module 5: Customer Onboarding & Ethical Culture in Financial Advisory
*   **Focus:** Ethics, compliance, AML/KYC, professional conduct.
*   **Challenge:** Navigating complex ethical situations and regulatory requirements.
*   **Solution:** Lead with integrity, robust AML/CFT controls, ethical decision-making.
*   **Key Topics:** Customer Onboarding & AML, Counter-Terrorism & Financial Crime, Ethics & Professional Conduct, Ethical Culture Building.
*   **Duration:** E-Learning (6 hours).
*   **CPD:** 6 Core FAA-FACPD Hours.

#### Module 6A (Elective): Financial Planning for Gig Economy Workers
*   **Focus:** Irregular income, self-employed planning, gig economy strategies.
*   **Challenge:** Irregular income, lack of CPF/benefits, market uncertainties.
*   **Solution:** Strategies for variable income, cash flow planning, retirement without CPF.
*   **Key Topics:** Gig Economy Landscape, Financial Planning for Variable Income, Retirement & Investment Planning.
*   **Duration:** 2 Days (14 hours).
*   **CPD:** 14 Non-core Supplementary Hours.

#### Module 6B (Elective): Retirement Planning for HNWIs and Business Owners
*   **Focus:** Retirement as business exit, accumulation/decumulation, succession integration.
*   **Challenge:** Timing exits, integrating succession, complex portfolios, multi-jurisdictional issues.
*   **Solution:** Integrate exit strategies, trust/insurance solutions, tax-efficient structures.
*   **Key Topics:** HNWI Retirement Framework, Estate Planning in Retirement, Product Structures & Solutions, Investment & Portfolio Planning.
*   **Duration:** 2 Days (14 hours).
*   **CPD:** 14 Non-core Supplementary Hours.
